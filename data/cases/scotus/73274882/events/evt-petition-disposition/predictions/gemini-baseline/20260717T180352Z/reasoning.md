## Legal Question & Standard
The petition presents a challenge to California's ban on ammunition feeding devices holding more than ten rounds (commonly referred to as "high-capacity magazines") and its retrospective application without compensation (a Takings Clause claim). Following the Supreme Court's decisions in *Heller* and *Bruen*, the core Second Amendment inquiry is whether the banned arms are in "common use" for lawful purposes, and if so, whether the state can demonstrate a historical tradition of analogous regulations.
