**Analysis**

1.  **Pro Se and Vexatious Litigator Status:** Gomez is proceeding pro se and has a history of vexatious litigation regarding these same underlying family law disputes, which drastically lowers the credibility and coherence of his petition.
2.  **Settled Law (Immunity):** The application of qualified immunity to the officer and absolute judicial immunity to the state judges and clerks involves straightforward applications of deeply settled law.
3.  **Jurisdictional Defect:** The Rooker-Feldman doctrine correctly bars lower federal courts from exercising appellate jurisdiction over state-court judgments, removing federal subject-matter jurisdiction over his core grievances.
