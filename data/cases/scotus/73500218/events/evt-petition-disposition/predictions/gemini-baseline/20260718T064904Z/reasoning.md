## Legal Question and Standard
The petitioner, Joan Farr, has filed a petition for a writ of mandamus. Under Supreme Court Rule 20, the issuance of an extraordinary writ like mandamus is "not a matter of right, but of discretion sparingly exercised." The petitioner must demonstrate that the writ will be in aid of the Court's appellate jurisdiction, that exceptional circumstances warrant the exercise of the Court's discretionary powers, and that adequate relief cannot be obtained in any other form or from any other court.
